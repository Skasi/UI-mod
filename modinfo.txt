	Name: UI mod, May 18. 2016
	Description: Improvements to the user interface with the aim of displaying more information in places where they matter.

	* More info in resource bar tooltips. (eg. influence/research generation efficiencies)
	* Artifact and Influence Card purchase/activation costs visible in quickbar.
	* More quickbar menus enabled by default. (cards, labor planets, defense targets)
	* Research tree unlock prices visible at second zoom level.
	* Star temperature displayed in tooltips.
	* Planet pressure display expanded to Active / Imported / Cap. (Active is new)
	* Planet tooltip now always shows population cap.
